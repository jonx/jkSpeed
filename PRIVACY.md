# Privacy Policy — jkSpeed

**Last updated:** March 25, 2026

## Data Collection

jkSpeed does **not** collect, store, or transmit any user data. The extension operates entirely locally in your browser.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used only to communicate with the current page when you click the extension icon.
- **webNavigation**: Used only to enumerate frames on the current page to detect videos inside iframes.

## Third-Party Services

jkSpeed does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, visit [jkn.me/chrome](https://jkn.me/chrome).
